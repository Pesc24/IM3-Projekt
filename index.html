ABOUT US

Is the air ready for your next run? Use our tool to find out. On this website you’ll find the best time slot to exercise outside. With one click, you can find the air pollution and temperatures of where you are. Your lungs will thank you!
A WebApp made by Pascal Linder and Thomas Buser as part of a university project for the university of Graubünden.
